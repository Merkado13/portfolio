Pink Goo

Oct, 2021

Main Role: Generalist Programmer

Technology:

Time: 3 months

Pink Goo is a vertical slice of an asymmetric VR armchair game in which pink goo characters must defeat a fire-breathing dinosaur while taking control of the islands. The game pits a virtual reality player, who plays the dinosaur, against up to 6 mobile players, who play the goo. The VR player must shout to be able to shoot fire out of his mouth to exterminate the goo threat, while the mobile players, on their mission to kill the dinosaur, must compete with each other to see who dominates the most islands. If the dinosaur survives, the VR player will be the winner.



To make the game more exciting, different power-ups will appear for both the dinosaur and the goos that will turn the game upside down, from the dinosaur being able to make a tsunami to the goos being able to hypnotize the dinosaur forcing him to dance if he wants to be able to see what is happening around him. In addition, there are several types of goo which have unique special abilities.



In this project I worked on:

Implementation of several goo and dinosaur power-ups.

Tutorial design and implementation.

VR Player height calibration tool.

Additional gameplay polish and balance.

Additional game design
