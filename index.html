About me


My name is Marcos Agudo. I'm a Spanish videogame programmer specialized in Gameplay and Gamefeel with +5 years of experience with engines such as Unity or Unreal, as well as game design and production, skills that have further helped me in the development of my work.


Knowing game mechanics are the language players use in order to communicate within the game, I deem it my responsibility to ensure this language is not only carefully considered, but meaningful.


Some insights of how I work are:

Programming at the service of design

I see programming as a means to realise an idea, therefore I always keep in mind a design oriented way to handle the gameplay systems I build.

Player movement is king

Movement is the first contact the players have with the game and what they will use throughout the gameplay. Moving around has to be fun and satisfying by itself.

Juice it or lose it

Gamefeel is a keystone of gameplay, it's what sells the action, if gameplay is a language, gamefeel is its expressiveness.

Let's communicate!

As a gameplay programmer it is necessary to maintain a constant flow of communication with designers and artists, let's talk, let's be on the same page!
