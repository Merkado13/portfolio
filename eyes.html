Child of my Eyes

Sep, 2020

Download on itch.io

Main Role: Programmer, Director

Technology:

Time: 7 days

"Child of my eyes" is a buss rush game with bullet hell hints where you face the internal grief that blooms after the loss of a loved one. Each boss represent a stage of grief, between each battle narrative unfolds touching on topics such as euthanasia or Catholic guilt.


The game was presented to the "Spanish Game Dev Jam II" following the theme "Duel" or "Grief"



As a gameplay programmer I worked on:

Implementation of the skull boss: sounds, movement and attacks.

Gameflow, from start to finish.

General gamefeel of the game.

Shooting Mechanic.


As a director I established the creative direction of the game as well as the narrative and the work pipeline for a team of six people.
