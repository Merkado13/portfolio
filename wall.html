Main Roles: Programmer, Director

Time: 5 days

"A W4ll Out" is a jam game about escaping a sarcastic AI's firewall with the help of a hammer that is able to break the fourth wall (literally...).


The objective of the game is to destroy all the walls numbered four that you encounter in order to break free, if you don't manage to beat all the levels in time you will return to the starting point, encouraging a speedrunning pace.


The theme of the game jam was double: loops and the forth wall.



As a gameplay programmer I worked on:

A parameterizable first person movement system with head bobbing

Screen breaking level transition

Gamefeel


As a director I was in charge of establishing a creative direction for the game as well as the pipeline of work for a team of six people. In addition, I contributed to the design of most of the levels.
